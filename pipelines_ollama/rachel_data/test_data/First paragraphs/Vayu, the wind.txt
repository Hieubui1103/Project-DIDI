 Everytime I finish my hot, hot bath. 
 
 
 My wet body fels so col, col 
 
 col. 
 
 
 What makes that hapen? 
 
 
 Vayu, the wind!

 The milk in my cup - to hot, to hot.
 
 
 But son it is ready for me to gulp.
 
 
 Who makes that hapen? 
 
 
 Vayu, the wind!

 The window curtains fluter and 
 
 gently brush my face. 
 
 
 Who makes it hapen? 
 
 
 Vayu, the wind! 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 3  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 A bolt of lightning far away. 
 
 
 Black clouds moving my way. 
 
 
 Who makes that hapen? 
 
 
 Vayu, the wind! 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 4  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Branches sway and leaves tremble. 
 
 
 Flowers gently fal. 
 
 
 Who did it al? 
 
 
 Vayu, the wind! 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 5  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Far from the house, we are playing,
 yet, I can smel the swets mother is
 
 preparing. 
 
 
 Who makes it hapen? 
 
 
 Vayu, the wind! 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 6  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 A glas tumbler on a windowsil, 
 crashes on the ground. 
 
 
 
 I am glad I was not around. 
 
 
 Who played this mischief? 
 
 
 Of course, it was Vayu, the wind! 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 7  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 A whistle blows. A train rols in.
 
 
 I canot se, but hear its din. 
 
 
 Who makes that hapen? 
 
 
 Vayu, the wind!
